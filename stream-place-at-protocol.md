## Stream.place

Video streaming infrastructure designed for the AT Protocol (ActivityPub) ecosystem.

### Use Cases
- Host video content on the fediverse/AT-Protocol
- Integrate with Bluesky, Mastodon, and other AT-P compliant platforms
- Decentralized video hosting for the open social web

### Related
- AT Protocol (formerly Bluesky)
- ActivityPub
- Fediverse video alternatives to YouTube

### Notes
- Part of the emerging AT Protocol video infrastructure
- Enables creators to stream/share video without centralized platforms
