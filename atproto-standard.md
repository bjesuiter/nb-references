## My Reference

### Summary

Standard Site is a specification and tooling for publishing long-form content on the AT Protocol (Authenticated Transfer Protocol). It provides a standardized way to create, share, and discover blog posts, articles, and other long-form content on the decentralized AT Protocol network.

**Key features:**
- Long-form content support for AT Protocol
- Interoperable content discovery
- Standardized post formats
- Cross-client compatibility

### Content

Useful for:
- Building AT Protocol tools with blog/article publishing
- Creating long-form content experiences on Bluesky/fediverse
- Interoperable content platforms on AT Protocol
- Following the standard for content portability
