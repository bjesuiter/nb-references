## Summary

**Lewitt Ray** is the first professional XLR microphone with **AURA sensor technology** - a sensor-based system that detects your distance to the mic and enables automatic voice balancing. Perfect for podcasts, streaming, and content creation.

## Key Features

### AURA Sensor Technology
- Detects your distance to the microphone (5-100 cm / 2-40 in)
- Automatically adjusts your sound for perfectly balanced recordings
- No need to worry about audio levels during performance

### MUTE by Distance
- Automatically mute when you step away from the mic
- Unmute when you approach
- Configurable thresholds: 15/30/45/60/80 cm (6/12/18/24/32 in)
- -70 dB attenuation when muted

### Physical Features
- **MUTE button** with clear visual feedback (works even when distance muting is active)
- Lock mode for safety
- Compact, camera-friendly design
- Magnetic popfilter stays securely in place
- Included shock mount reduces handling noise
