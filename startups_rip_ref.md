## My Reference

### Summary
A curated collection of failed Y Combinator startups (2005-2026) with deeply researched retrospectives and rebuild playbooks. The site tracks 1700+ dead or acquired startups, organized by batch, industry, and outcome. The premise: "Startups die. Ideas don't."

### Featured Examples
- **Posterous** (YC S08) - Dead simple blogs by email → acquired
- **Loopt** (YC S05) - Social mapping service → acquired by Snap
- **Webvan** (1999) - Online grocery delivery → failed, but idea revived by Instacart
- **Parse** (YC S11) - Backend-as-a-service → acquired by Facebook, open-sourced
- **LendUp** (YC W12) - Socially responsible fintech → inactive

### Key Insight
Many failed startup ideas get revived by other companies. The site analyzes what went wrong and what ideas survived.
