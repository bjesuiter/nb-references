## Summary

Mobbin is a design inspiration platform featuring over 1,150 iOS & Web apps and 580,800+ screens. New content added weekly.

## Features

- **Design Discovery:** Find design patterns across thousands of real apps
- **User Journeys:** Explore entire flows with videos showing transitions, micro-interactions, and animations
- **Prototype Mode:** Interactive hotspots to walk through flows step-by-step
- **Figma Integration:** Copy designs directly to Figma with their plugin
- **Collections:** Save favorite designs and upload own screenshots
- **Comments:** Take notes when saving for future reference
