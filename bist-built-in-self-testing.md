### Summary

BIST (Built-In Self Testing) - A testing architecture where testing capabilities are built directly into the system itself, rather than being external.

### Key Points

- Self-testing capabilities integrated into the product/system
- Often used in security-critical systems
- Enables continuous validation without external test infrastructure
