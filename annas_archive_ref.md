## My Reference

### Summary
Anna's Archive is a volunteer-run project building "the library of the future" (books, papers, magazines, etc.). They coordinate work via GitLab issues with a **bounty system** — contributors can claim tasks and earn money for completing them. Bounties are awarded to the first person to complete a task.

### How It Works
- Browse open issues sorted by priority on their GitLab
- Comment on a ticket to claim you're working on it
- Complete the task to receive the bounty
- Examples of bounty tasks:
  - **Seeding torrents** — $200/month for seeding all torrents
  - **Metadata mirrors** — $200+/month for running mirror sites
  - **Scraping large collections** — variable bounties
  - **Professional book scanning** — bounty-based
