**FlashAI Voice Agents** by FlashLabs is a voice cloning solution for building AI voice agents. Claims high-quality voice synthesis with local processing capabilities.

- **Voice agent integration** — Built for conversational AI
- **High quality output** — Professional-grade voice synthesis
