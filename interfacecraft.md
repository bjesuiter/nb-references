## Summary

**Interface Craft** is a design resource by Josh Puckett (ex-Wealthfront, Dropbox) for creating thoughtful, timeless interfaces.

### Philosophy
- Software that feels *right*
- Interfaces that feel timeless
- "Unreasonable level of consideration"
- Products that are loved, not just functional

### Content
5 initial collections with:
- Articles
- Videos
- Interactive playgrounds
- Examples
- Curated references

### About the Author
**Josh Puckett** - 20 years designing products:
- Wealthfront
- Dropbox
- 100+ designers mentored (now at Apple, Browserbase, etc.)

> "I apply what I learned from him daily, and you should too."

### Quote
"Software is rapidly getting easier to make. And most of what gets made will probably be fine. Functional, but forgettable. Made quickly without much thought. This is for people who want to make something else."
