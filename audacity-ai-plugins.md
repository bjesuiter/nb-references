## Available AI Effects

- **Music Separation** — Split audio into stems (Drums, Bass, Vocals, Other)
- **Noise Suppression** — Reduce background noise, optimized for speech
- **Music Generation** — Generate music or continuations using MusicGen LLM
- **Whisper Transcription** — Transcribe audio to text via OpenAI Whisper
- **Audio Super Resolution** — Upsample audio for higher fidelity
