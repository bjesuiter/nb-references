## Random Anruf Checklist

When receiving a likely spam call:

1. **Adresse, Firma und Webseite anfragen** — Ask for address, company name, and website
2. **Datenauskunft nach DSGVO anfragen** — Request data access request under GDPR
3. **Fragen woher sie die Daten kennen** — Ask where they got your data from

---

## Notes
- Use in German when speaking to German callers
- Requesting GDPR data access often stops spam calls quickly
